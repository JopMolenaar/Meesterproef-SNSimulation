Social Media Simulator

Welcome to the Social Media Simulator

This simulator allows users to configure the structure of social networks and news articles, and visualizes the spread of fake news. This project supports the research of Responsible IT at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences into the role of AI in the dissemination of misinformation.
